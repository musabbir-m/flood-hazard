Our donate plan

We carefully designed to help 3 necessary aspect of life of the affected community.

Food for a family

Affected families will be supported with basic food requirments including dry foods, baby foods and safe water

Shelter for a family

During the ongoing hazard families will be provided with temporary shelter support and during the post-hazard they will be provided with support for maintanace of their house and finance to construct a new house for the worst-case scenario

Education for the child

Children will be provide with primary hygiene knowlede to stay safe in the flood. They will be also supported with learning materilas during and after the flood
